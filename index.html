Warnock for GA

The Man: Warnock is the youngest pastor to lead from MLK, Jr.'s pulpit. He has excellent policy stances, which is reflected in broad endorsements from the DSCC and BradyPac to the progressive: NARAL Pro Choice, End Citizens United, and Democracy for America. He's a justice warrior and proud ally to the LGBTQ+ community, all in the deep south. We could fill pages why Warnock may be the best person we support. But our representative democracy is 2.5 centuries behind electing Black leaders. With someone like Warnock need we say more?

The Race: Warnock runs in a special election against four others. If one candidate gets 50% of votes, they will be seated this Nov. Otherwise, the top two winners will have a run-off in January where the entire nation of decent Americans will rally around Warnock's win. Just today, polls show him surging into first place; he may just nab the 50% in Nov. And if not, he will definitely make the runoff. A Warnock win unseats Kelly Loeffler, the very Senator who told America not to worry about COVID in February while secretly dumping her vulnerable stock holdings. She is a criminal.

•Giving Impact: Our support in GA has huge ballot impact on: (i) two Senate races (Warnock / Ossoff); and (ii) major opportunities to win flippable state chambers with an excellent slate of local female candidates, including many Black women. These winnable races aren't getting the national coverage they need, which is why 46P will bring our might.
